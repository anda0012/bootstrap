Tips for You and Your Community

Get Involved
  • Contact your local politicians. Ask them what they are doing to protect your community's environment
  • Participate in activities in your comunity geared towards the environment
Volunteer opportunities
Be Energy Efficient
  • Switch off lights when not in use
  • Unplug unused electronics
  • Check your home's insulation. Seal window cracks, change or clean filters
Click for more tips
Minimize Waste
  • Eliminate the use of plastic bags
  • Learn proper recycling methods
  • Clean up litter in your neighbourhood as a community activity
  • Avoid purchasing overly packaged products
Recycling tips
Travel Green
  • Carpool with friends and coworkers
  • Bike or walk to work or school if you live in walking distance
  • Take public transit when convenient
  • Try to be conscious of your carbon footprint
Carbon footprint
Conserve Fresh Water
  • Don't let the faucet run when brushing
  • Make sure none of your taps drip
  • Run your washing machine and dishwasher on full loads
More tips
Buy Local Food
  • Support your local farmers
  • Reduce food travel miles
  • Get fresher and tastier food in return
